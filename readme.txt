
GAME RULES:

- The game has 2 players, playing in rounds. There are 2 dices.
- In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his ROUND score. ROUND score is the total of score shown on both the dices.
- BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all his ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
- The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn
- The first player to reach 30 points on GLOBAL score wins the game
- Players can set GLOBAL score also. Type the score you want to set and press SET FINAL SCORE button. 
- A player looses his ENTIRE score when he rolls two 8 in a row. After that, it's the next player's turn.
